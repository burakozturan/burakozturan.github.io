I am a PhD Student at Network Science Institute, Northeastern, advised by [Prof. David Lazer](https://lazerlab.net/)
I am  in computational social science methods. He uses social networks and NLP to examine digital trace data. His current research revolves around the converging and diverging patterns of COVID-19 disinformation networks across social media platforms. Before joining the Network Sciences Institute, Burak completed his master's degree in Data Science (from Konstanz University) with his thesis titled "The COVID-19 Infodemics on Turkish Twittersphere". He has been a data-scientists in the GMF-supported project that measured the elite public opinion on Turkish-American Relations
